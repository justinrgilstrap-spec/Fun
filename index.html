    How do I get my Timeline file?

    Timeline now lives on your phone, so you export it from the Google Maps app:

    1. Open the Google Maps app and tap your profile picture → Your Timeline.
    2. Open Settings (⋯) and choose Export Timeline data; save the .json.
    3. Move that file to this computer, then drop it above.

    More detail in Google's Timeline help.

    How do I get my Google Photos location data?

    Use Google Takeout to export just Photos:

    1. At takeout.google.com, click Deselect all, then check only Google Photos.
    2. Export, download, and unzip the archive on this computer.
    3. Drag the whole Google Photos folder from inside it onto the dropzone above (or use "click to choose files" to multi-select the .json files directly).

    Only each photo's location and timestamp are read — nothing is uploaded, and the actual photo/video files are ignored.
